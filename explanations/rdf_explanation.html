üîó What is an IRI/URI?

Every "thing" in RDF needs a unique address - like a website URL!

1
Instead of just saying "John"...
We say: http://university.com/people#John
2
Why? Because there might be millions of "Johns" in the world!
The URL makes it UNIQUE - like a passport number for data.
